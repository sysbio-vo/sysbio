Research directions

Our research area includes following topics:

Transcriptomics

studying human organ systems in health and disease with classical molecular biology techniques as well as high-throughout methods

Bioinformatics

integrative analysis of multi-omics data, gene regulatory networks reconstruction, GRID and Cloud computing in biosciences

Environmental metagenomics

biodiversity of built environment (mass transit systems, hospitals, universities, offices, and schools), AMR detection, whole-genome sequencing

Proteomics

high-throughput mass-spectrometry
