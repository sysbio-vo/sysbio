How to get to us

Send us email to the address listed below or check our social media. For in-person visits, please, schedule a meeting beforehand, as we might work remotely due to coronavirus pandemic.

We are located in Holosiivskyi District not far from Feofaniya.

You can easily get to us from VDNH metro station using public transport such as route bus 444 or 507, and trolleybus 11.

Get off at the Instytut bdzhilnytstva stop and then cross the street via pedestrian crossing, beware of the traffic lights!
